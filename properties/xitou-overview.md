# 蟬說：溪頭 (Chanshuo: Xitou)

## Setting

蟬說：溪頭 sits in 南投 (Nantou) at the edge of the famed 溪頭 forest — Taiwan's iconic bamboo and conifer forest reserve. The property is built around immersion in the surrounding ecology: forest walks, evening soundscapes, a quieter pace than urban life allows.

## What guests come for

- **Forest immersion** — the bamboo and conifer ecosystem is the headline draw. The property is positioned to make full ecology programming accessible.
- **Year-round operation** with seasonal accents — summer ecology programming is most distinctive
- **Distance from city noise** — for guests seeking a genuine reset
