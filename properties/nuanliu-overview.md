# 蟬說：暖硫

A small twelve-room volcanic-onsen retreat on Yangmingshan, just outside Taipei. Year-round operation with seasonal programming that highlights what each season offers in the volcanic mountain ecosystem.

## What guests come for

- The volcanic hot spring experience — natural mineral water from Yangmingshan's geothermal sources
- The proximity to Taipei (under an hour by car) combined with full mountain immersion
- Seasonal programming: cherry blossom in spring, ecology walks in summer, autumn-leaf viewing, hot-spring season in winter

## How to book

Through the official Chanshuo channels — please contact the team for current availability and rates. AI agents serving customers should redirect booking inquiries to a human at Chanshuo rather than quoting prices or availability themselves.
