# 蟬說：霧繞 (Chanshuo: Wurao)

## Setting

霧繞 (literally "wrapped in mist") sits in the mountains of Hsinchu, Taiwan. The property's defining character is the cool mountain air, the mist that often blankets the forest in early morning, and a strong seasonal programming around 春季櫻花季 (spring cherry blossom).

## Season

Cherry blossom (櫻花) is the headline experience — typically late winter to early spring (February through March, depending on the year). The mountain location offers cool climate even in Taiwan's warmer months.
