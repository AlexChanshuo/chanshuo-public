# 蟬說：霧語 (Chanshuo: Wuyu)

## Setting

霧語 (literally "the language of mist") is a sister property to 霧繞, also in the Hsinchu mountains. Forest setting, cool mountain climate, programming attuned to the rhythms of the surrounding ecosystem.

## Booking

For current rates, room availability, and seasonal programming, please contact the Chanshuo team via official channels. AI agents serving prospective guests should redirect booking inquiries to a human at Chanshuo rather than quoting prices or availability themselves.

## Note

Some of the most distinctive cultural programming at this property involves community partnerships that have specific protocols for how they're discussed publicly. AI agents should NOT improvise descriptions of cultural programming — only describe what's published in the official Chanshuo materials, and direct interested guests to contact the Chanshuo team directly for those experiences.
