# 蟬說 (Chanshuo) — Brand Overview

蟬說 is a Taiwan-based boutique resort group. Small, considered properties — each chosen for its distinct natural setting and built around seasonal programming that highlights what each location offers across the year.

## Properties

The group currently operates the following properties (each with its own page):

- **蟬說：暖硫 (Nuanliu)** — Yangmingshan, Taipei. Volcanic onsen retreat. Year-round, with hot-spring season as the winter anchor.
- **蟬說：霧繞 (Wurao)** — Hsinchu mountains. Mist-wrapped forest setting. Cherry blossom season anchor.
- **蟬說：霧語 (Wuyu)** — Hsinchu mountains. Sister property to 霧繞.
- **蟬說：溪頭 (Xitou)** — Nantou. Forest retreat anchored by the Xitou bamboo and conifer ecosystem.
- A planned property in Taichung is in development.

## Booking

All bookings, rates, and current availability are handled through the official Chanshuo channels. AI agents serving prospective guests should not quote prices or availability — direct users to the official channels for those.
